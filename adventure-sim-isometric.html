adventure sim - isometric prototype

Adventure Sim is an Adventurer Simulation Town-builder game for Android written in Java with Android Studio. The game uses the Entity-Component-System architecture. This prototype uses the isometric perspective and uses behavior trees for AI.

HOW TO PLAY

You are the Warrior. Tap anywhere on the screen to move. Movement is implemented with A* pathfinding. The White Mage will try to stay near you.

GATHERING MATERIALS

Tap on the stones, branch, and berries to put in inventory. A slime decided to attack the White Mage. She will first run closer to you then attack the slime if it's still attacking her.

INVENTORY

Press the bag icon to view, use, or discard your items.

CRAFTING

Press the pickaxe and hammer icon to view your crafting recipes and craft.

CHOPPING

Once you crafted an axe, you can chop trees for lumber.

COMBAT

Slimes roam around and attack nearby characters. Tap them to attack. The White Mage will heal you if your HP falls below 50% if she has MP.
